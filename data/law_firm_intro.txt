Smith & Johnson Law Firm, established in 1995 in New York City, is a full-service law firm that provides comprehensive legal services to businesses of all sizes. 
The firm specializes in corporate law, intellectual property law, real estate transactions, employment law, and litigation. 
Over the past 25+ years, Smith & Johnson has successfully represented clients ranging from small startups to Fortune 500 companies in various industries including technology, healthcare, and finance. 
The firm's mission is to deliver high-quality, strategic legal advice while maintaining the highest standards of integrity, professionalism, and client service.
