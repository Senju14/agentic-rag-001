Anderson & Lee Consulting Group, founded in 2002 in San Francisco,
is a leading management consulting firm serving clients across North America, Europe, and Asia.
The firm provides strategic advisory services in the areas of business transformation,
digital innovation, operations optimization, and organizational development.
For more than two decades, Anderson & Lee has worked with Fortune 100 corporations,
mid-sized enterprises, and emerging startups in industries such as retail, manufacturing,
and financial services.
Its mission is to empower businesses with data-driven insights, actionable strategies,
and sustainable solutions that drive long-term growth and competitive advantage.
